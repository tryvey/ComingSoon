About Me

Jamie is a UX/UI Designer with a passion for problem solving and creating engaging experiences. He has 15+ years design experience spanning a wide range of industries and mediums. With 5+ years hands-on experience in user research, visual storytelling, UX/UI design, rapid prototyping, and user testing for rich desktop and mobile applications.

Whether it is developing new digital products, or iterating on existing ones, Jamie practices user centered design principles basing design decisions on user needs and business goals.
